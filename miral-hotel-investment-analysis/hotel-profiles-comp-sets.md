## 5. Comp Set Construction Guide

### Rules for Miral Hotel Comp Sets

**Always match:**
1. Chain scale (luxury vs. upscale vs. midscale — do not mix)
2. Geography (Yas Island vs. Saadiyat Island vs. Abu Dhabi city — RevPAR levels differ)
3. Demand profile (theme park/leisure vs. business/MICE vs. beach resort)
4. Room count (within ±40% of subject property)

**Recommended comp sets by property:**

| Subject Property | Recommended Comp Set |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| The WB Abu Dhabi (5★) | W Abu Dhabi – Yas Island, Hilton Abu Dhabi Yas Island, Park Hyatt Saadiyat, St. Regis Saadiyat |
| Hilton Abu Dhabi Yas Island (5★) | W Abu Dhabi, The WB Abu Dhabi, Radisson Blu Yas Island, Crowne Plaza Yas Island |
| Crowne Plaza Yas Island (4★) | Radisson Blu Yas Island, Yas Island Rotana, Hilton Abu Dhabi Yas Island (with caveats) |
| Radisson Blu Yas Island (4★) | Crowne Plaza Yas Island, Yas Island Rotana, W Abu Dhabi (upper comp) |
| Park Inn Yas Island (3★) | Centro Yas Island, DoubleTree Yas Island, budget Abu Dhabi city hotels |
| Saadiyat Island resorts | Park Hyatt Saadiyat, Rixos Saadiyat, St. Regis Saadiyat, comparable 5★ Dubai beach resorts |

**Do NOT mix:**
- Yas Island leisure hotels with Abu Dhabi downtown business hotels (different demand profile)
- Full-service and select-service properties (GOPPAR not comparable)
- Theme park-adjacent and pure beach resort (different ADR drivers and seasonality)

## 6. Brand Fee Structures Reference

*Approximate ranges — [VERIFY current agreements as fees are negotiated individually]*

| Brand / Operator | Base Management Fee | Incentive Fee | Franchise Fee (rooms) | Notes |
|-----------------|--------------------|--------------|-----------------------|-------|
| Hilton | 2–3% of total revenue | 8–10% of GOP above threshold | 5–6% | WB, Hilton, DoubleTree |
| IHG | 2–3% of total revenue | 8–10% of GOP | 5–6% | Crowne Plaza, Staybridge |
| Marriott / W Hotels | 3% of total revenue | 10% of GOP | 6–7% | Higher fees; premium brands |
| Rotana | 2–2.5% of total revenue | 6–8% of GOP | N/A (mostly managed, not franchised) | Regional operator |
| Radisson | 2–2.5% of total revenue | 6–8% of GOP | 4–5% | Radisson Blu, Park Inn |

**Important modelling note:**
- Management fees are an operating expense (above NOI)
- Franchise fees are also above NOI
- Combined fee drag: 8–12% of total revenue for branded full-service hotels
- For cap rate purposes: NOI = GOP - FF&E Reserve - Fixed Charges (management + franchise fees
  are included in departmental expenses, NOT below NOI)
